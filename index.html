About Us

Learn More About Us

Thank You For

Supporting Our Schools

New 2 You of Lansing is a not-for-profit upscale resale store which specializes in gently used clothing, shoes, accessories, housewares, books, toys, and furniture, along with many other items for all areas of your home or office.

The store exists to provide tuition assistance to families with children who attend Calvin Christian School in South Holland and Lansing Christian School in Lansing. Along with tuition assistance, we also strive to provide our local community with reasonably priced, high-quality goods.

Our store is staffed by a significant number of regular volunteers from supporting churches, as well as family members of the students who attend these schools. Day-to-day operation is managed by our director and day manager(s). Our Board of Directors meets regularly to oversee operations. The items for sale are donations, which are gratefully accepted during all regular store hours. Over 23 years ago, several Christian school parents from both Calvin Christian School in South Holland and Lansing Christian School in Lansing shared a concern about the rising cost of Christian education. Together they had the idea of starting a thrift store to raise money to provide tuition assistance to families with children at their schools and to serve as a witness for the Lord in the surrounding community. After many months of planning, their vision for the future resulted in the formation of what was at that time known as “Harvest Thrift Center”.
